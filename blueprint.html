Blueprint

A blueprint is an item used in production.
Blueprints are made by researching certain technologies as well as by additional research on a given Blueprint. Research done on a Blueprints can be focused on a specific trait of the blueprint as well as its overall stats. A blueprint represents a design for an object with certain properties.
By researching on a blueprint, new blueprints can be created, the properties of which will be altered.
Blueprints can be used to make Blueprint Copies, the next step in the production process.
A blueprint is a physical item an can thus be stolen by piracy.

A Blueprint has an unlimited life span and is the master blueprint of its associated technology. This means that losing a blueprint results in the inability to create additional Blueprint Copies.
