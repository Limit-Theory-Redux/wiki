Blueprint Copy

An assembly chip is an item used in production.
An assembly chip is created from a blueprint. An assembly chip is essentially a "crafting recipe"; you need an assembly chip together with the correct resources to produce an object. The assembly chip is consumed in the process.
The idea behind this is that the original technology creator has the (unique) blueprint, and can use that to create assembly chips, which can then be used or sold.
The blueprint can then be considered as some form of patent, and an assembly as a limited use production license.
