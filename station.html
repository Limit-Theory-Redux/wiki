Station

Stations are large stationary objects that act as points of interest within a star system as they offer a location to conduct trade and other important interactions.  A station represents a centralized hub for the economy.  While docked at a station a ship can change its loadout, buy and sell equipment, ships, and other goods as well as create and accept contracts.

Some stations also offer inventory space that can be rented and used to store an assortment of valuables which can be retrieved later.

Stations are owned by a faction and are often used to stake a claim on the surrounding area.  

Stations can be equipped with different modules.  These modules require large volumes to operate efficiently.  Some examples of modules include:

  • Research and Development labs
  • Factories
  • Item Storage
