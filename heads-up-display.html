Heads Up Display

The Heads Up Display or HUD provides the player information about the environment and surroundings in the Limit Theory universe. The HUD elements can be customized in size, position, and color.

 

Top Left: System name, Zone name, Owner of zone
Bottom Left: Top down view of part of the system, Weapon Group sorting tool
Center Top: Contacts, System Map, Transactions, Unknown
Center: Speed indicator, Crosshair, Energy capacitor, Ship health
Center Bottom: Energy settings indicator
Top Right: Target Health indicator, Transaction information
Bottom Right: Scanner
