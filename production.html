Production

The production process starts in one of two ways, either on the research screen where a blueprint can be created, or when a blueprint is purchased from the market.  The blueprint itself can be used in production, though this is not recommended, or can be used to create a Blueprint Copy each of which have a player defined time limit.  A Blueprint Copy is then sent to a factory along with the required resources for production.  The factory then takes the blueprint copy and resources and begins production.  Production continues until a number of items is reached, the blueprint copy expires, or the factory runs out of resources.
