Hardpoints

Hardpoints are general categories that allow particular equipment types to be mounted to a ship.

Types of Hardpoints

Dedicated Hardpoints

  • Capacitor
  • Communicator
  • Computer
  • Engines
  • Hull
  • Sensor

General Hardpoints

  • Armor
  • Cloak
  • Drone Rack
  • Cargo/Berth
  • Shield
  • Weapons Bay
  • Weapons Turret
