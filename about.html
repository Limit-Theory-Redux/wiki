In brief, Limit Theory Redux is a first person space sandbox game with real time strategy elements.  Explore a massive open world where boundless opportunity is waiting to be discovered.  Trade with other ships in a working simulated economy where prices change based on supply and demand.  Conquer your enemies as a commander of a massive fleet or as a single fighter.  Disrupt the flow of supplies or hire underlings to do it for you.  Build the world you've always dreamed of in this thought provoking, fun single player universe where your choices matter.

 

Known Supported Systems
Mac, Linux, and Windows (64 bit)

Supported Input Devices
Game pad, joystick, mouse and keyboard
